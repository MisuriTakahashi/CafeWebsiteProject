About us

Established on the eve of the pandemic, Tsambayan Milktea was founded on a simple passion for quality refreshments. Recognizing the growing demand for milk tea, we set out to bridge the gap between affordability and premium taste.

Our name, a blend of 'Tsa' (Tea) and 'Tambayan' (a community hangout), reflects our mission: providing a welcoming space for milk tea lovers to gather and enjoy their favorite flavors.
